SXSW

.CO

.CO is a global top-level domain (TLDs) targeting those who want a unique web address and to join a community of innovators and entrepreneurs.

SXSW was a brand and awareness event for .CO. The company likes to celebrate current customers by making them part of the .COmmunity. A custom coloring book, focusing on all aspects of the .CO experience, was created as a giveaway. Social graphics, a landing page and onsite promotional graphics were created.
