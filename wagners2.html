AdAge Fact Pack

Neustar

For the second Fact Pack, we needed original photography and more charcters than the immediate Wagner family, to give the Wagner campaign legs. I headed up the casting, shot list, props and scheduling. After two weeks in L.A., we has original stock for characters. Since this Fact Pact focused on travel, media/entertainment, automotive and mobile tech, we used this format to show off the company's data in these industries and showcase the solutions that support them.

AdAge's Consumers On The Go is a guide to consumer segments with facts on travel, media/entertainment, automotive and mobile tech. This consumer-focused Fact Pack offered key stats on major demographic segments including age groups (Millennials, Gen X, Boomers, 70-plus) and ethnicity/race with insights into the behavior and preferences of different segments.
