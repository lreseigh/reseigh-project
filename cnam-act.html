The Telecom Econonmic Stimulus Act campaign

TARGUSinfo

To play off the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act, ahigh impact direct mail campaign was created for TARGUSinfo’s Caller Name Services (CNAM). To encourage carriers to switch, the company wanted to offer a reduction in cost for one year and revenue share for three years.

The Telecom CNAM Economic Stimulus Act had three parts. The executive order, the passed Act to sign into law and the formalized Act with all the details.

This campaign went to a small target audience within the telecom industry, who wer enot currenly TARGUSinfo customers. Costs to the company were low per set and only a small fraction of the ROI produced from one new customer.
