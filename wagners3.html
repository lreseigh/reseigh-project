AdAge Fact Pack 2016

Neustar

This is AdAge's annual guide to marketers, media and agencies. We used this format to tell a marketing omnichannel, device and personalization story that revolves around Jason Wagner's birthday party. Since this Fact Pact is kept as a reference guide for the ad industry all year, it was important to keep the Wagner story relevant and memorable.

For this campaign, I was the art director, designer and project manager. The head copy writer and I collaborated to make sure the visuals worked with the visuals from the previous photoshoot.
