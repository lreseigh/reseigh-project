You are .nyc campaign

.nyc

.nyc is a generic top-level domains (gTLDs) that offers New Yorkers one of the first citywide domains.

They wanted a fresh take on social and display ads. The web address is only for New Yorkers, so the campaign focused on stressing the love and pride of being a New Yorker.
