Online Lead Quality Summit

TARGUSinfo

The Online Lead Quality Summit (OLQS) was first introduced as an exclusive small (60 ppl) gathering of TARGUSinfo customers to discuss online lead buying, selling and aggregation. TARGUSinfo was the leader in the space, but wanted to create a community to help the industry grow. The first year was such a success that a registration fee was added and it was opened to prospects. Word spread on the high quality of content, amazing networking and social atmosphere. ROI paid for the event and produced millions of dollars in deals flourishing which might have never happened without the event.

Creative work for OLQS included save the date cards, direct mailers, high impact mailers, onsight signage, onsight books, stage signage, print and display advertising, emails, landing pages, animation, presentation templates and custom decks, post show marketing and a mini-site.

OLQS ran for four years until being rebranded. Images here reflect the evolution of the event.
